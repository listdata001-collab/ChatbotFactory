🧠 AI Prompt: BotFactory AI Chatbot Platformasi (O'zbek tilida)
Siz — tajribali Python Flask dasturchi. Quyidagi talablarga mos, sodda va professional Telegram AI chatbot platformasini yozing. Hamma narsa o'zbek tilida bo'lishi kerak.

📌 Umumiy talablarning qisqacha mazmuni:
Backend: Python Flask (MVC arxitektura)
Frontend: Bootstrap 5 + Jinja2 templates
Database: SQLAlchemy ORM + PostgreSQL/SQLite
AI: Google Gemini API (gemini-2.5-flash)
Messaging: python-telegram-bot
Auth: Flask-Login + bcrypt
Server: Gunicorn
